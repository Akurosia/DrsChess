STRATEGY

  1. Go to the goal (blue) and turn camera so that the goal is on the bottom row
  2. Find the knight walking west/left: if it moves 1 or 2 tiles, the left column is safe. Otherwise, the second column from left is safe
  3. Find the knight walking north/up: if it moves 1 or 2 tiles, the upper row is safe. Otherwise, the second row from top is safe
  4. Count your total moves, go into the safe column and then in the direction of the safe row. If you have moves left, go right
  5. If you cannot reach the safe row (5 steps special case): look where the knights walking up and down go. Depending on where they go, either go into: [r2, c1], [r3, c2] or [r4, c3]
  6. To resolve the first edict, go into the safe column and then walk down (retrace your steps)

CONTROLS

  • Start the game with the button on top
  • Move by clicking on a tile, or with WASD or arrow keys
  • You can move, whenever you would be able to in the game (as accurately as I could get it to work)
